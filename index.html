    Back Story

    The initiative started with blending Charities & P2E NFT games into the same pool. Following the recent devastating events in our artists home country Ukraine, the ever changing struggles & livelihood of pandas was an idea which initially started with just the donation aspect but slowly emerged into a P2E game as we want to provide value for our holders as well. Vision emerged from providing a helping hand and raising awareness globally to holders getting a chance to give back via the P2E game so anyone and everyone can give back to the less fortunate ones…

    Introduction

    LostPanda gives a chance to help donating without the donation coming directly from your pockets. How? Introducing the first Charity based P2E game where holders can choose to donate their earnings and the less fortunate to have a chance at earning a quick buck. After giving away 25% of mint proceeds in order to directly make a difference we will offer a game experience that aims at making the world more diverse by players coming from all walks of life for the same vision ; Model is simple,”Help the less fortunate” which is a win for everybody. Holders / Players / Charities / Wildlife / Ukraine and the list goes on…

    What is Lost Panda?

    Lost Panda is a collection of 2,388 adorable little Panda residing on the Ethereum blockchain as a unique NFT made with Combination of remarkable traits soon to be shifted to their home in the metaverse where users can earn whilst having a choice of giving back…
